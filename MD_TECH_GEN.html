About MD TECH GEN

MD TECH GEN is a professional tool for generating valid credit card numbers for testing purposes. These numbers are created using the Luhn algorithm and are not real, meaning they cannot be used for transactions.

  • Purpose: Designed for developers to test payment gateways, validate forms, or simulate transactions in controlled environments.
  • Luhn Algorithm: Ensures generated numbers pass checksum validation, mimicking real card structures.
  • BINs: Use custom or random BINs to target specific banks or regions. Our database includes major issuers for realistic testing.
  • Card Types:
    • Visa: Starts with 4, 16 digits
    • MasterCard: Starts with 51-55, 16 digits
    • Amex: Starts with 34 or 37, 15 digits
    • Discover: Starts with 6011 or 65, 16 digits
  • Legal Notice: This tool is for educational and testing purposes only. Misuse for illegal activities, such as carding, is strictly prohibited and traceable.
